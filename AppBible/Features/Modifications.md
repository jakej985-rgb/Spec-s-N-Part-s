# Modifications Feature

---

## Purpose
Track custom engine, suspension, wheels, interior, and exterior modifications for each vehicle.

---

## Core Functionality
- Log modification details (category, name, date, cost, notes).
- Attach photos and technical specs.
- Track which modifications are bolt-on vs. permanent.
- Calculate total modification costs.
- Share modification lists with forums or friends.

---

## Modification Categories
- Engine (turbo, supercharger, headers, ECU tune)
- Suspension (lowering springs, coilovers, sway bars)
- Wheels & Tires (upgrade, sizing changes)
- Drivetrain (differential, transmission tune)
- Exhaust (cat-back, full system)
- Interior (seats, steering wheel, gauges)
- Exterior (body kit, paint, spoiler)
- Electrical/Lighting (LEDs, audio)
- Other

---

## UX Flow
1. Open Modifications screen for a vehicle.
2. See list of all modifications with photos and cost.
3. Tap "Add Mod" to create new record:
   - Category dropdown
   - Name, description, date installed, cost
   - Upload photos (before/after)
   - Notes (performance gain, bolt-on, warranty impact)
4. Edit or delete past records.
5. View total mod cost summary.

## Integration
- Links to Parts page for parts related to mods (filters by category).
- Maintenance tracking for mod maintenance (e.g., turbo oil change).

---

## Acceptance Criteria
- User can add modification with photos and details.
- Total modification cost is calculated and displayed.
- Modifications persist and sync across devices.
- User can browse all mods for a vehicle.
