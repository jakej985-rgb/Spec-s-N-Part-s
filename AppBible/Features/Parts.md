# Parts Feature

---

## Purpose
Browse OEM and aftermarket parts catalog, save wishlist, and quickly jump to vendor links for purchasing.

---

## Core Functionality
- Search parts by vehicle, part number, or category.
- View part details: specs, fitment notes, images, compatibility.
- Cross-reference OEM vs. aftermarket alternatives.
- Save parts to wishlist for future purchase.
- Direct links to vendor store pages (Amazon, RockAuto, eBay, dealer).

---

## Parts Categories
- Engine (gaskets, valves, injectors, turbo)
- Suspension (springs, shocks, control arms, bushings)
- Braking (pads, rotors, lines, fluid)
- Wheels & Tires
- Exhaust (manifold, cat, muffler, resonator)
- Cooling (radiator, fans, hoses, thermostat)
- Electrical (alternator, starter, battery, wiring)
- Interior (seats, doors, trim, gauges)
- Exterior (bumpers, fenders, mirrors, body panels)

---

## Data Sources
- API integration with parts databases (e.g., RockAuto, EPC, Edmunds).
- User can manually add custom or rare parts.

---

## UX Flow
1. Open Parts screen.
2. Search or filter by vehicle and category.
3. Tap part to view details:
   - Images, specs, fitment notes
   - OEM number and aftermarket alternatives
   - Vendor links with pricing
4. Add to wishlist (saved locally and synced).
5. Tap vendor link to open shop (in-app browser or external).

---

## Wishlist
- Bookmark parts of interest for later.
- Organize by project or vehicle.
- Share wishlist link with friends/mechanic.
- Set price alerts (optional premium feature).

---

## Maintenance Integration
- When logging fluid change, suggest compatible filters and fluid SKUs.
- Link from Maintenance > brake service → show brake pad options for that vehicle.

---

## Acceptance Criteria
- Parts search returns results relevant to the vehicle and category.
- Vendor links work and redirect to product pages.
- Wishlist persists and syncs.
- Part details display correctly (images, specs, fitment).

---

## Future Ideas
- Price comparison across vendors.
- Inventory alerts (in stock/out of stock).
- Integration with shopping cart (add multiple parts and buy in one checkout).
- Reviews and ratings for parts.
