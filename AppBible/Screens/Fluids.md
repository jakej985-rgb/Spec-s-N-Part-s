# Fluids

---

## Purpose
- Display recommended fluid specifications (oil, coolant, transmission, brake) for the selected vehicle and allow quick reference during service.

---

## Widgets
- Fluid summary card (type, capacity, spec#)
- Change interval recommendations and notes
- Links to compatible parts (filters to Parts page)

---

## Actions
- Switch vehicle context for fluid specs.
- Copy or share fluid specs for shop use.
- Add fluid change to maintenance schedule.

---

## Uses Classes
- Vehicles
- SpecsRepository (factory fluid specs)
- MaintenanceRecord (to schedule fluid changes)

---

## Acceptance Criteria
- Fluid specs load for the selected vehicle and match factory data.
- Users can schedule fluid changes directly from this screen.

---

## Future Ideas
- Show local availability of recommended fluids and compatible part numbers.
- Interval calculator based on usage patterns.
