# Home Screen

---

## Purpose
- Provide a dashboard with quick access to the currently selected vehicle's status, upcoming maintenance, and common actions.

---

## Widgets
- Current vehicle card (image, VIN, mileage)
- Mileage and fuel summary
- Upcoming maintenance and reminders
- Quick actions (Add maintenance, Start trip, Log fill-up)
- Recent activity feed (recent services, parts ordered)

---

## Actions
- Switch current vehicle.
- Jump to maintenance, parts, or details from quick actions.
- Log mileage and fuel entries.

---

## Uses Classes
- Vehicles
- TripLog
- MaintenanceRecord
- FuelLog

---

## Acceptance Criteria
- Dashboard shows up-to-date vehicle info and upcoming reminders.
- Quick actions navigate correctly and pre-fill context when applicable.

---

## Future Ideas
- Widgets that can be rearranged by the user.
- Integrate with OBD device for live telemetry.
