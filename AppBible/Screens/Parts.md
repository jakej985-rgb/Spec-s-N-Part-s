# Parts Page

---

## Purpose
- Present parts catalog and part numbers for the selected vehicle; allow ordering, bookmarking, and cross-referencing.

---

## Widgets
- Parts search with filters (category, compatibility)
- Part detail panel (part number, images, fitment notes)
- Cart/ordering quick-add and saved lists
- Cross-reference table (OEM vs aftermarket)

---

## Actions
- Search and filter parts by vehicle compatibility.
- View part details, add to wishlist or cart.
- Open vendor link to purchase.
- Save part to project or maintenance record.

---

## Uses Classes
- PartsCatalog (search, compatibility)
- Vehicles (for fitment checks)
- OrdersService (cart & purchase links)

---

## Acceptance Criteria
- Search returns compatible parts for the selected vehicle.
- Part detail shows correct OEM numbers and images.
- Wishlist and add-to-cart actions persist.

---

## Future Ideas
- Price comparison across vendors.
- Integrate with local dealers for availability.
